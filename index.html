House Rules

A few things to know before you check in, so the stay is smooth for you and the guests after you.

  • No cancellation or refund policy is available.
  • Additional guests will be charged ₹1,000 per person per night.
  • Check-in: 1:00 PM. Check-out: 10:00 AM.
  • Early check-in and late check-out are not permitted.
  • A refundable security deposit of ₹3,000 is required at check-in. The deposit may be forfeited if house rules are violated or if any damage/breakage occurs on the property.
  • Caretakers are available on call between 10:00 AM and 5:00 PM. Guests are requested to address their queries during these hours.
  • Kitchen usage: ₹500 per day/night. We do not provide a gas connection or stove; an induction cooktop with basic induction-compatible cookware is available under this charge.
  • Barbecue setup: ₹500 per use. We provide a barbecue grill, skewers, and 2 kg of charcoal.
  • Valid government-issued photo ID is required for all guests at check-in.
  • Towels are provided every 2 days per guest for multi-night bookings.
  • Bedsheets are changed every 3 days for multi-night bookings.
  • Cleaning is scheduled between 11:00 AM and 1:00 PM for multi-night bookings. Requests for cleaning outside the scheduled housekeeping hours cannot be accommodated.
  • Pool access is available from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM. Pool usage is strictly prohibited after 10:00 PM.
  • Outdoor music is permitted until 10:00 PM. After 10:00 PM, music may be played indoors at a moderate volume.
  • Decorations are permitted only under the supervision and approval of the caretaker. Decorations on interior walls, furniture, or other surfaces are not allowed. Unauthorized decorations may result in additional charges.
  • Power outages from MSEB are beyond our control. Inverter backup is available for approximately 2–3 hours. No refund or compensation will be provided for electricity outages.
  • Respectful behavior toward staff and management is mandatory. Rude, abusive, or inappropriate behavior may result in immediate eviction from the villa without refund.
  • Pets are welcome. Please do not allow pets on beds, sofas, or in the swimming pool. Pet owners are responsible for cleaning up after their pets. A maximum of 2 pets are allowed free of charge; any additional pets will be charged ₹1,000 per pet per stay.
  • Any damage to the property, furniture, fixtures, amenities, or decor will result in additional charges.
  • Smoking is permitted in outdoor areas only. Smoking inside the villa is strictly prohibited.
