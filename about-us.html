StellarStays Story

Founded in 2020, Stellar Stays was created with a simple vision – to bring together exceptional hospitality and smart property management while creating value for both property owners and guests. We partner with villa owners to maximize the potential and returns of their properties, while offering guests thoughtfully managed stays designed around comfort, quality, and memorable experiences.

From carefully curating each home to managing every detail of the guest journey, our focus is on delivering seamless service and meaningful experiences. Through trusted operations, attention to detail, and a passion for hospitality, we create stays that feel effortless, memorable, and worth returning to. At Stellar Stays, we believe great hospitality creates lasting value for everyone.
